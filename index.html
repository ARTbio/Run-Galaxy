  1. Prerequisite: a Google account / Gmail account
  2. Connect to Google Cloud Engine

  4. Enter your Gmail mail address and password
  5. Review conditions and accept
  6. Inscription Form:
  7. Entreprise / Company : put anything like "Perso" or "foo/bar"
  8. Ajouter une carte de paiement / Add credit Card or optionally Ajouter un compte bancaire / Bank account
  9. You are in for a free trial of 12 months / 300 $
  10. go to your Google Cloud Console to control your spin off / control your Virtual Machines
